Alien Cuisine Guide: What to Eat on Mars 🚀

Overview

Living on Mars would be very different from living on Earth. Since farming in soil is difficult there, astronauts may grow food using special methods like hydroponics.

Hydroponic Greens 🌱

Hydroponic greens are vegetables grown in water with nutrients instead of soil. This method helps plants grow in places where normal farming is not possible.

Examples

- Lettuce
- Spinach
- Kale
- Herbs (basil, mint)

Why It’s Good for Mars

- No soil needed
- Water can be reused
- Plants grow faster
- Provides important vitamins

Conclusion

Hydroponic farming could help astronauts grow fresh food on Mars and stay healthy while living on another planet.
